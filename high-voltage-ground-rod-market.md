<p><p>The High Voltage Ground Rod market comprises three main types: Double Tongue Ground Rod, Single Tongue Ground Rod, and Screw Compression. Double Tongue Ground Rods are designed with two tongues at the top, enabling easy connection of multiple grounding wires. Single Tongue Ground Rods have a single tongue, suitable for connecting a single grounding wire. Screw Compression Ground Rods use a screw mechanism for securing the wire to the rod, providing a secure and reliable connection. Each type serves specific purposes and meets diverse requirements in electrical grounding applications.</p></p>
