<p><p>The BT substrate market comprises various types such as WB BGA, WB CSP, and FC CSP. WB BGA refers to the wafer bumping ball grid array packaging technique which provides high-density interconnections for various semiconductor devices. WB CSP, also known as wafer-level chip scale package, is a packaging technology that allows multiple chips to be integrated onto a single substrate. FC CSP stands for flip chip chip scale package, offering enhanced electrical performance by flipping the chip and connecting it directly to the substrate. These different types cater to diverse requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
