<p><p>The solid lithium battery market comprises two main types: polymer-based solid-state lithium batteries and solid-state lithium batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes. Polymer-based solid-state batteries use a polymer electrolyte to transfer lithium ions between electrodes, providing enhanced safety and stability compared to traditional liquid electrolyte batteries. On the other hand, solid-state lithium batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes use a solid electrolyte material, offering even higher energy density and longer cycle life. Both types of solid lithium batteries are being extensively researched for their potential applications in various industries.</p></p>
