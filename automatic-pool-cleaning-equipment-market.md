<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Pool Cleaning Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaning equipment market is categorized into three types: suction-side pool cleaners, pressure-side pool cleaners, and electric pool cleaners. Suction-side pool cleaners operate by being connected to the pool's suction port and use the pool's filtration system to suck up dirt and debris. Pressure-side pool cleaners utilize the pool's return jets to create pressure and push debris into a collection bag. Electric pool cleaners are self-contained units that operate independently using electricity, and they have a motor and filter to clean the pool.</p></p>
