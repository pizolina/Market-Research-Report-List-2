<p><p>The electric bus charging infrastructure market includes various types, namely depot charging system, city charging system, and column charging system. Depot charging system involves installing charging stations at bus depots, allowing buses to recharge during their idle time. City charging system focuses on setting up charging stations in public locations throughout the city, enabling buses to recharge during their routes. On the other hand, column charging system involves the installation of charging pillars at specific locations, allowing buses to recharge when parked. These systems aim to provide convenient and efficient charging solutions for electric buses.</p></p>
