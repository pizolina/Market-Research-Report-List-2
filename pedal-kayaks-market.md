<p><p>Pedal kayaks are a popular choice for water enthusiasts due to their hands-free operation. There are two main types in the market: push pedal and rotational pedal kayaks. Push pedal kayaks involve a back and forth motion, where the paddler uses their legs to push the pedals and propel the kayak forward. On the other hand, rotational pedal kayaks involve a circular motion, mimicking the movement of a bicycle. Both types offer advantages in different situations, providing users with options based on their preferences and needs.</p></p>
