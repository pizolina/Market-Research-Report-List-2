<p><p>Silicon impression materials are commonly used in dentistry for making dental impressions. Light body silicon materials are more fluid and have low viscosity, making them ideal for capturing intricate details in areas with narrow spaces. Medium to heavy body silicon materials have a higher viscosity and are used to create a stable base for the impression. Both types of silicon impression materials play crucial roles in ensuring accurate and precise dental impressions.</p></p>
