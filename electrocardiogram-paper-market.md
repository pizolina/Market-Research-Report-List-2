<p><p>The electrocardiogram (ECG) paper market consists of two main types: single roll and Z fold. Single roll ECG paper is typically sold in a continuous roll format, allowing for easy feeding into ECG machines. It is commonly used in hospitals and clinics due to its convenience and compatibility. On the other hand, Z fold ECG paper is designed with a zigzag pattern, allowing for easy folding and storage. This type of paper is often used in portable ECG machines or for personal use at home.</p></p>
