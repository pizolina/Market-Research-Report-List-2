<p><p>The liquid analyzer market offers various types of analyzers for different purposes. Turbidity analyzers measure the clarity of liquids, while pH/ORP analyzers determine the acidity or oxidation-reduction potential. TOC and COD analyzers assess the organic and chemical content. Titro analyzers analyze concentration levels. Conductivity analyzers measure the conducting ability of liquids. Chlorine analyzers determine chlorine levels, and dissolved oxygen analyzers measure oxygen dissolved in liquids. These analyzers are crucial in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing for quality control and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
