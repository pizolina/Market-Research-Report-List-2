<p><p>An air knife is a mechanical device used to blow off, clean, or dry items or surfaces using a high-velocity and controlled air stream. It is an essential tool in various industries such as food processing, automotive, electronics, pharmaceutical, and packaging. An air knife typically consists of a high-velocity fan or blower that forces air through a narrow slot, creating a curtain of air with consistent pressure and velocity.</p><p>The global air knife market is witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 11.7% during the forecast period. There are several factors contributing to this growth. Firstly, the demand for efficient and cost-effective cleaning and drying solutions across industries is driving the adoption of air knives. They offer advantages such as reduced energy consumption, faster processing time, improved product quality, and reduced maintenance costs.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology are leading to the development of more efficient air knives with enhanced features and capabilities. For instance, the integration of sensors and advanced control systems allows for better regulation of air pressure and velocity, optimizing performance and enhancing efficiency. Furthermore, the rising focus on sustainability and environmental concerns is promoting the adoption of air knives as they reduce the use of compressed air or other resources compared to traditional cleaning or drying methods.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing automation and industrialization across various sectors are also fueling the growth of the air knife market.
