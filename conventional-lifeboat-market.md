<p><p>Conventional lifeboats are divided into two main types: closed lifeboats and free fall lifeboats. Closed lifeboats are designed for evacuation during emergencies and are fully enclosed, ensuring the safety of passengers from harsh weather conditions and water. On the other hand, free fall lifeboats are specialized for rapid descent from a platform or ship into the water in case of emergencies. These lifeboats allow for quick evacuation and are equipped with advanced technology to ensure a safe landing, even in extreme conditions.</p></p>
