<p><p>The waste heat recovery boiler market can be categorized into three main types. Firstly, water tube boilers are used to generate steam or hot water by circulating water through tubes that are surrounded by hot gases. Secondly, fire tube boilers use the heat from hot gas flowing inside the tubes to transfer it to the water, thus producing steam. Lastly, other types of waste heat recovery boilers include heat exchangers, economizers, and condensing economizers, which operate based on different mechanisms to recover waste heat and increase energy efficiency.</p></p>
