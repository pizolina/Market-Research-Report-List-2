<p><p>The instant water heater market is divided into two types: electric and gas. Electric instant water heaters are powered by electricity and use heating elements to provide hot water immediately. They are easy to install and affordable but may have higher energy costs. On the other hand, gas instant water heaters operate using natural gas or propane as fuel, heating water through a gas burner. They offer faster heating and lower operating costs but require proper ventilation and more extensive installation.</p></p>
