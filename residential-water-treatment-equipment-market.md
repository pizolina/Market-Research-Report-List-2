<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU</li><li>POE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POU (Point-of-Use) refers to water treatment equipment that is installed specifically at the point where water is consumed or used, such as under the kitchen sink or on a countertop. It provides localized water treatment for a specific tap or appliance, ensuring that the water is safe and clean for immediate use. </p><p>POE (Point-of-Entry) refers to water treatment equipment that is installed at the entry point of a residential water supply, most commonly at the main water line where it enters the house. This equipment treats and purifies the entire water supply, ensuring that all faucets and appliances throughout the house receive clean and safe water.</p></p>
