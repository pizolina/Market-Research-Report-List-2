<p><p>Retaining compounds are used to enhance the strength and integrity of assemblies. Medium strength retaining compounds offer a moderate level of bonding capability, suitable for applications requiring disassembly with hand tools. High strength retaining compounds provide a strong bond and are designed for applications requiring excellent resistance to shock, vibration, and chemical exposure. Low strength retaining compounds provide a weaker bond, allowing for easy removal and disassembly. Each type of retaining compound caters to different assembly requirements, ensuring optimal performance and durability.</p></p>
