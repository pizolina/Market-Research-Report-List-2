<p><p>The elastic pin coupling market offers three types of couplings: cylindrical shaft hole, tapered shaft hole, and short cylindrical shaft hole. Cylindrical shaft hole couplings have a cylindrical shape and are designed to fit onto cylindrical shafts. Tapered shaft hole couplings have a tapered shape, allowing them to be used with shafts that have tapered ends. Short cylindrical shaft hole couplings are similar to the cylindrical shaft hole couplings but have a shorter length. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries requiring elastic pin couplings for their machinery.</p></p>
