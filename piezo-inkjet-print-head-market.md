<p><strong>The Piezo Inkjet Print Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Inkjet Printhead</li><li>Non-MEMS Inkjet Printhead</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Piezo Inkjet Print Head market is categorized into two types: MEMS Inkjet Printhead and Non-MEMS Inkjet Printhead. MEMS Inkjet Printhead implements Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems technology, which combines microscopic mechanical elements, sensors, actuators, and electronics on a common silicon substrate. This allows for precise ink droplet formation and accurate printing. On the other hand, Non-MEMS Inkjet Printhead does not use this technology and instead relies on other methods, such as thermal or bubble ejection, to expel ink droplets onto the paper surface.</p></p>
