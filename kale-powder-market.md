<p><p>Kale powder is available in two main market types: air dry and freeze dry. Air dry kale powder is produced by removing moisture from kale leaves using air circulation, resulting in a powdered form that retains most of its nutritional value. Freeze dry kale powder, on the other hand, involves freezing kale leaves and then removing the moisture through a process called sublimation, which preserves more nutrients than air drying. Both options offer convenient ways to incorporate the nutritional benefits of kale into various recipes and diets.</p></p>
