<p><p>Tensiometer market types include force sensor tensiometers, bubble pressure tensiometers, drop volume tensiometers, spinning drop tensiometers, and others. Force sensor tensiometers measure the surface tension by applying force on the liquid surface. Bubble pressure tensiometers determine the surface tension based on the pressure required to form a bubble in a liquid. Drop volume tensiometers measure the surface tension by analyzing the volume and shape of a drop put on a substrate. Spinning drop tensiometers determine the surface tension by observing the drop's spinning behavior. Other types of tensiometers may use different techniques to measure surface tension.</p></p>
