<p><p>The video intercom system market is divided into three types: door station, video intercom master, and indoor units. The door station is placed outside the building and allows visitors to communicate with residents through video and audio. The video intercom master is the central device that manages and controls the system, providing features like call forwarding and recording. Indoor units are placed inside the building and enable residents to communicate with visitors and unlock doors remotely. These three types together form the video intercom system market, providing enhanced security and convenience for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
