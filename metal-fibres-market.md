<p><p>The metal fibers market comprises various types such as vacuum deposition, coating metal powder with binders, sputter coating, and electroless coating. Vacuum deposition involves depositing metal atoms onto a substrate through vaporization and condensation. Coating metal powder with binders involves the application of a binder onto metal particles to improve their adhesion. Sputter coating utilizes plasma-generated ions to deposit metal films onto a substrate. Electroless coating employs a chemical reaction to deposit a layer of metal on a surface without the need for an electric current.</p></p>
