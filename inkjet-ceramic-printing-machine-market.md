<p><strong>The Inkjet Ceramic Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Printing</li><li>Dye-sublimation Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Inkjet Ceramic Printing Machine market, there are two types of printing technologies: UV Printing and Dye-sublimation Printing. UV Printing involves the use of ultraviolet light to dry and cure the ink, resulting in a durable and vibrant print on ceramic surfaces. On the other hand, Dye-sublimation Printing uses heat to transfer dye onto the ceramic, resulting in a long-lasting and high-quality image. Both methods offer efficient and precise printing solutions for ceramic products.</p></p>
