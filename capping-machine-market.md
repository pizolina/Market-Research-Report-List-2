<p><p>The capping machine market can be categorized into two types: automatic and semi-automatic machines. Automatic capping machines are designed to operate without human intervention, providing a fully automated process. These machines are capable of handling high production volumes and offer efficient and precise capping solutions. On the other hand, semi-automatic capping machines require some level of human involvement, such as manually placing the cap on the bottle before the machine applies the necessary sealing mechanism. These machines are suitable for smaller-scale production or applications where manual control is preferred.</p></p>
