<p><p>A Storage Dewar is a specialized vacuum flask used to store and transport cryogenic materials such as liquid nitrogen, oxygen, and argon. It has a double-walled construction with an inner vessel that holds the cryogenic liquid and an outer shell that provides insulation. Storage Dewars are commonly used in laboratories, hospitals, and research facilities.</p><p>A Dry Shipper, on the other hand, is a type of vacuum flask designed for the safe transportation of biological samples at ultra-low temperatures. It uses a combination of vacuum insulation and absorbent material to maintain the temperature inside and prevent the samples from coming into direct contact with liquid nitrogen.</p><p>The Storage Dewar and Dry Shipper market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for cryopreservation of biological samples, pharmaceuticals, and food products. The ability of these products to maintain low temperatures for extended durations without the need for electricity or additional refrigeration systems has propelled their adoption in various industries.</p><p>Additionally, the growing prevalence of chronic diseases and the subsequent advancements in medical research have further augmented the demand for Storage Dewar and Dry Shipper systems in the healthcare sector. These systems are crucial for the safe storage and transportation of biological samples used in diagnostics, research, and development of therapies.</p><p>Key trends in the Storage Dewar and Dry Shipper market include the development of advanced materials and technologies to enhance the insulation and durability of these systems.
