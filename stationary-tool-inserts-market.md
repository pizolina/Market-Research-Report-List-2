<p><p>The stationary tool inserts market includes various types such as carbide inserts, CBN inserts, and ceramic inserts. Carbide inserts are made of carbide material and are commonly used for cutting and shaping applications. CBN inserts, on the other hand, are made of cubic boron nitride and are primarily used for machining hardened steels and cast iron. Ceramic inserts are made of ceramic materials and are known for their high heat resistance and excellent wear resistance properties. These inserts are widely used in the metalworking industry for improved productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
