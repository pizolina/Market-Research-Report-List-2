<p><p>Hydrogen brazing is a process used in joining metal parts. It includes two types of markets: dry hydrogen brazing and wet hydrogen brazing. Dry hydrogen brazing involves the use of hydrogen gas without any moisture. This method offers advantages like cleanliness, minimal oxidation, and precise control over the brazing process. On the other hand, wet hydrogen brazing involves the use of hydrogen gas with added moisture. This method offers benefits like increased heat transfer, improved flow properties, and enhanced metallurgical bonding. Both market types aim to provide efficient and effective metal joining solutions.</p></p>
