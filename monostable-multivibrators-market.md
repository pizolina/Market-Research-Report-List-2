<p><p>Monostable multivibrators, also known as one-shot multivibrators or monoflops, are electronic circuits that generate a single output pulse when triggered by a trigger pulse. These circuits have two stable states: a stable state with no output pulse and an unstable state with an output pulse. Once triggered, the circuit moves from the stable state to the unstable state, and after a certain time, it automatically returns to the stable state. Monostable multivibrators find applications in various electronic devices such as timers, flip-flops, counters, and pulse generators.</p><p>The global monostable multivibrators market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.80% during the forecast period.
