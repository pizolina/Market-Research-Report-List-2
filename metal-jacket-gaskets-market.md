<p><p>Metal jacket gaskets are a type of mechanical seals used in various industrial applications to provide an effective sealing solution. These gaskets are made up of a soft filler material (such as graphite, PTFE, or other materials) surrounded by a metal jacket, usually made of carbon steel or stainless steel. The metal jacket enhances the strength and resilience of the gasket, while the filler material provides the sealing properties.</p><p>The metal jacket gaskets market is driven by a range of factors, including increasing demand from industries such as oil and gas, power generation, chemical processing, and automotive. These industries rely on metal jacket gaskets to prevent leakage and ensure efficient operation of various equipment and pipelines.
