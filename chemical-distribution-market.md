<p><p>Chemical distribution involves the transportation and sale of chemicals to various industries. Pipelines are used to transport large volumes of chemicals over long distances efficiently. Containers, such as intermediate bulk containers (IBCs), are employed for shipping smaller quantities of chemicals. Barrels are commonly used for the storage and transportation of liquid chemicals. Sacks, on the other hand, are used for packaging solid chemicals and are often distributed in smaller quantities. These different market types cater to the diverse needs of chemical distribution, ensuring safe and efficient delivery of chemicals to end-users.</p></p>
