<p><strong>The DNA Sequencing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing</li><li>Next Generation DNA Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DNA sequencing equipment market consists of two major types: Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing and Next Generation DNA Sequencing. Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing employs the Sanger sequencing method, where a DNA sample is amplified and labeled with fluorescent tags before being passed through a gel electrophoresis system. This method is accurate but time-consuming. On the other hand, Next Generation DNA Sequencing uses high-throughput technologies to simultaneously sequence millions of DNA fragments. This method is faster and more cost-effective, allowing for rapid analysis of large genomes.</p></p>
