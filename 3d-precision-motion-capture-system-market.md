<p><strong>The 3D Precision Motion Capture System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical System</li><li>Inertial System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical system is a type of precision motion capture system that uses cameras to track the positions and movements of markers placed on a subject's body. It provides high accuracy and is commonly used in industries such as animation, sports analysis, and medical research. The inertial system, on the other hand, utilizes sensors attached to the body to measure motion and rotation. This system is suitable for capturing movements in real-time and is often used in gaming, virtual reality, and sports training. Lastly, the "other" market comprises alternative motion capture technologies like electromagnetic and mechanical systems, which cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
