<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Power System</li><li>Central Power System</li><li>Hybrid Power System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Lighting System market consists of three types: Self-Contained Power Systems, Central Power Systems, and Hybrid Power Systems. Self-Contained Power Systems are standalone units with integrated power sources, such as batteries, that can provide emergency lighting during power outages. Central Power Systems utilize a central battery system that powers multiple emergency lights and exit signs. Hybrid Power Systems combine the features of both self-contained and central power systems by using a combination of batteries and central power sources to provide emergency lighting.</p></p>
