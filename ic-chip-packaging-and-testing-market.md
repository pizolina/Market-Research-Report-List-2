<p><p>IC chip packaging and testing market involves different types of packaging technologies. Some of the common types of packaging include Ball Grid Array (BGA), Land Grid Array (LGA), System in Package (SiP), Flip Chip (FC), and others. BGA packaging uses an array of solder balls on the bottom of the chip, LGA packages have pins on the chip's bottom surface. SiP combines multiple chips in a single package. FC packaging directly connects the chip to the substrate. The "Others" category includes various other packaging types such as Quad Flat No-Lead (QFN), Chip-scale package (CSP), and Dual In-line Package (DIP), among others.</p></p>
