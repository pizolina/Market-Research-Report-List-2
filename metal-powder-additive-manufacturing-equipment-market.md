<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing Equipment refers to the use of metal powder as a raw material for creating three-dimensional objects through a layer-by-layer printing process. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) is a type of equipment that uses a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder particles together. Electron Beam Melting (EBM), on the other hand, utilizes an electron beam to melt and solidify the metal powder layers. The "Others" market includes alternative technologies or variations of these processes. These equipment types enable the production of complex metal parts with high precision and customization capabilities.</p></p>
