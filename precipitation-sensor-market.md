<p><ul><li>Tipping Bucket Precipitation Sensor</li><li>Weighing Precipitation Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precipitation sensor market includes two main types: the tipping bucket precipitation sensor and the weighing precipitation sensor. The tipping bucket sensor measures rainfall by using a small bucket that tips and empties every time it collects a specific amount of precipitation. On the other hand, the weighing precipitation sensor measures rainfall by using a sensitive balance that calculates the weight of the collected precipitation. Both types of sensors are used to accurately measure and monitor rainfall in various applications, such as weather forecasting, agriculture, and flood control.</p></p>
