<p><p>Beverage processing equipment market consists of two main types, namely, raw material processing equipment and filling and packaging equipment. Raw material processing equipment is used in beverage production to transform ingredients into the desired form for further processing. It involves activities like sorting, grinding, filtering, and pasteurization. On the other hand, filling and packaging equipment is responsible for filling beverages into containers, such as bottles or cans, and packaging them for distribution. This includes processes like filling, labeling, sealing, and palletizing. Both types of equipment are crucial for the efficient production and distribution of beverages.</p></p>
