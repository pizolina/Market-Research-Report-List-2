<p><p>Gear pumps are positive displacement pumps that operate by using gears to transfer fluids. Firstly, the increasing demand for gear pumps in the oil and gas industry, particularly in upstream operations, is driving market growth. Gear pumps are used for various operations, such as crude oil transfer, hydraulic fracturing, and drilling mud circulation.</p><p>Moreover, the growing chemical industry is another key factor propelling the market growth of gear pumps. These pumps are used for handling different chemicals, including corrosive, flammable, and hazardous liquids, due to their ability to handle high-pressure and high-temperature fluids.</p><p>Additionally, rising awareness about energy-efficient and environmentally friendly pumps is fostering the demand for gear pumps. These pumps are known for their high efficiency, minimal energy consumption, and reduced emissions, making them favorable in various industries.</p><p>In terms of the latest trends, the integration of advanced technologies like IoT and automation in gear pumps is gaining traction. Manufacturers are continuously focusing on improving the durability and performance of gear pumps to meet the evolving industrial requirements.</p><p>Overall, the Gear Pumps Market is projected to witness substantial growth with a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period.
