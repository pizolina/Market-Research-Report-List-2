<p><strong>The Peltier Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multistage Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peltier Modules Market is classified into two types: Single Stage Module and Multistage Module. A Single Stage Module consists of a single layer of thermoelectric material, allowing it to achieve cooling or heating in one direction. On the other hand, a Multistage Module comprises multiple layers of thermoelectric materials, enabling it to cool or heat in both directions. While Single Stage Modules are generally used in simpler applications, Multistage Modules are preferred in more complex situations where precise temperature control is required.</p></p>
