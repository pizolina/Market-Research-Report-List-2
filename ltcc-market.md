<p><p>LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) technology is used in various markets including LTCC Components, LTCC Substrates, and LTCC Module market. </p><p>LTCC Components refer to individual parts fabricated using the LTCC process, such as capacitors, inductors, resistors, and antennas. These components offer miniaturization, high frequency capabilities, and good electrical properties.</p><p>LTCC Substrates are flat ceramic plates used as a base for mounting electronic components. These substrates provide good thermal conductivity, low loss, high frequency operation, and hermetic sealing.</p><p>LTCC Modules combine multiple components on a single module, providing a complete functionality. These modules are compact, lightweight, and offer improved performance and reliability.</p></p>
