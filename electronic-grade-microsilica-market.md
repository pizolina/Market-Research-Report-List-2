<p><p>Electronic Grade Microsilica is a high-performance additive used in electronic materials and devices. It is available in two main types: Angular and Spherical. Angular microsilica particles have irregular shapes, providing a larger surface area, which enhances particle-to-particle bonding and improves material strength. Spherical microsilica particles have a spherical shape, allowing them to flow more easily and pack more densely, resulting in improved material compactness. Both types serve different purposes based on specific material requirements, and their utilization helps enhance the performance and reliability of electronic materials and devices.</p></p>
