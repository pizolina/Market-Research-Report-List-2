<p><p>The power tool electric commutators market consists of different types of commutators, namely hook type commutator, groove commutator, and others. Hook type commutators have hooks at the ends of the segments that connect with the brushes. Groove commutators have grooves on the surface of the segments to hold the brushes. The "others" category includes different designs and variations of commutators that do not fit into the hook or groove type. These commutator types play a crucial role in the functioning of power tools by facilitating the flow of electric current.</p></p>
