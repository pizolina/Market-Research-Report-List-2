<p><p>Traction transformers are used in electric trains for converting electrical energy from the grid into suitable voltage levels. There are two main types of traction transformers: core type and shell type. </p><p>Core type traction transformers have a magnetic core that helps to enhance energy efficiency and reduce losses. They are compact, lightweight, and widely used in high-speed trains. </p><p>On the other hand, shell type traction transformers have an outer shell made of magnetic materials, providing better cooling and insulation. They are commonly used in suburban and regional trains. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and efficient operations of electric trains.</p></p>
