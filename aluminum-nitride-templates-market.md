<p><p>The aluminum nitride (AlN) templates market consists of two main types: AlN templates on sapphire and AlN templates on silicon. AlN templates on sapphire involve the growth of a thin layer of AlN on a sapphire substrate, offering advantages such as high thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. On the other hand, AlN templates on silicon involves depositing an AlN layer on a silicon substrate, allowing for the integration of AlN onto silicon-based devices, offering benefits like improved thermal management and enhanced performance. Both market types cater to different applications and industries.</p></p>
