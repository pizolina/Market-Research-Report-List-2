<p><strong>The Electric Loaders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Electric Loader</li><li>Hybrid Electric Loader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric loaders market includes two main types: full electric loaders and hybrid electric loaders. Full electric loaders solely rely on electric power for operation, without the need for any fossil fuels. These loaders are highly efficient, emission-free, and offer reduced noise levels. On the other hand, hybrid electric loaders combine electric power with an internal combustion engine, providing more flexibility and longer operating hours. These loaders can be powered by electricity or fossil fuels, offering a balance between environmental sustainability and extended range. Both types cater to the increasing demand for eco-friendly and efficient loaders in various industries.</p></p>
