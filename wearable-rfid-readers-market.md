<p><p>The Wearable RFID Readers market includes different types of RFID systems. LF RFID (Low Frequency) system operates at a frequency of 125 kHz and is mainly used for access control applications. HF RFID (High Frequency) system operates at a frequency of 13.56 MHz and is commonly used for payment and identification purposes. UHF RFID (Ultra-High Frequency) system operates at a frequency range of 860-960 MHz and is suited for tracking and logistics. Other types refer to RFID systems that may operate at different frequencies or have specific features based on unique requirements.</p></p>
