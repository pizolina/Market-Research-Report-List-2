<p><p>The Surface Mount System (SMS) market comprises various types, including Placement, Inspection, Soldering, Screen Printing, and Others. Placement involves the accurate placement of components onto PCBs for assembly. Inspection refers to the visual and automated inspection of PCBs to detect defects or inconsistencies in the manufacturing process. Soldering involves the process of creating a permanent bond between components and the PCB. Screen Printing involves the application of solder paste onto the PCB before component placement. Lastly, the Other category includes additional processes and equipment used in surface mount technology that do not fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
