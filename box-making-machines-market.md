<p><p>Box making machines are classified into two types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require human intervention for certain tasks, such as loading and unloading materials, adjustments, and quality checks. These machines offer flexibility and lower cost compared to fully-automatic ones. Fully-automatic machines, on the other hand, are fully automated and do not require human intervention once they are set up. They are faster and more efficient, making them suitable for high-volume production. The choice between these two types depends on the production requirements, budget, and level of automation desired.</p></p>
