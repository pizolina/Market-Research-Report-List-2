<p><p>The aerospace and military fiber optic market includes single-mode and multi-mode fiber optic types. Single-mode fibers have a narrow core size, allowing them to transmit data over longer distances with higher bandwidths. They are suitable for long-range communication and are commonly used in aerospace applications. On the other hand, multi-mode fibers have a larger core size and can transmit data over shorter distances but with lower bandwidths. They are often used for short-range communication within military applications. Both types cater to specific needs within the aerospace and military industries.</p></p>
