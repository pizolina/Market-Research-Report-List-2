<p><p>The Sleep Apnea Diagnostic Devices Market includes two main types: screening type devices and stereotyping type devices. Screening type devices are used to initially identify potential sleep apnea cases and evaluate its severity. These devices are portable and can be used at home to monitor breathing patterns, blood oxygen levels, and heart rate during sleep. Stereotyping type devices are more advanced and are utilized in sleep labs or clinics to provide a comprehensive diagnosis of sleep apnea, including determining the type of apnea and recommending appropriate treatment options.</p></p>
