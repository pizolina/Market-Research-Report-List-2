<p><p>The marine deep cycle battery market encompasses two main types of batteries: Flooded Lead Acid Battery and Lithium Ion Battery. Flooded Lead Acid Batteries are traditional batteries that contain a liquid electrolyte. They are known for their reliability and cost effectiveness, but they require regular maintenance to replenish the electrolyte. On the other hand, Lithium Ion Batteries are more advanced and offer longer service life, higher energy density, and faster charging. Although they are more expensive, they require less maintenance and have become popular due to their superior performance.</p></p>
