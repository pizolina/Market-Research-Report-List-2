<p><p>The viscoelastic damper market consists of various types of dampers used for controlling vibrations in structures. Flat dampers are thin sheets made of viscoelastic material and are commonly used in buildings to reduce vibrations caused by wind or earthquakes. Cylindrical viscoelastic dampers are cylindrical in shape and are usually installed in bridges and tall structures to absorb and dissipate energy from dynamic loads. Viscoelastic damping walls are designed with layers of viscoelastic material and are used to reduce vibrations in buildings or structures subjected to strong winds or vibrations.</p></p>
