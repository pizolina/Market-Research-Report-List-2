<p><p>The Aircraft Black Box market consists of two main types: Flight Data Recorder (FDR) and Cockpit Voice Recorder (CVR). The FDR records and stores flight parameters like altitude, speed, and heading, as well as other important aircraft data during the flight. The CVR, on the other hand, captures all the audio conversations and sounds in the cockpit, providing crucial records of pilot communications and aircraft noises. These black boxes are essential for accident investigation and enhancing flight safety by understanding the sequence of events leading to an incident or crash.</p></p>
