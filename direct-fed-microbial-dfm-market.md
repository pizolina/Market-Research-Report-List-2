DFM market types include bacteria, yeast, and others. Bacteria-based DFM products consist of beneficial strains such as Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium, which aid in digestion and boost the immune system. Yeast-based DFM products, usually Saccharomyces cerevisiae, enhance fiber digestion and promote gut health. Other DFM market types may include enzymes or fungi that help break down complex nutrients. These DFM supplements offer a natural and safe approach to improve animal nutrition and performance.</p></p>
