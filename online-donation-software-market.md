<p><ul><li>Cloud based</li><li>On Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based online donation software refers to software that is hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet. Users do not need to install or maintain any software on their own systems as everything is managed remotely. On the other hand, on-premise online donation software is installed and operated on local servers within an organization's premises. All data is stored locally, and the software requires on-site management and maintenance. Both types of software allow organizations to accept donations online, but the difference lies in the hosting and management infrastructure.</p></p>
