<p><p>The Automatic Packaging Machinery market is classified into two main types: Fully Automatic Packaging Machinery and Semi-Automatic Packaging Machinery. Fully Automatic Packaging Machinery refers to machines that can complete the entire packaging process without human intervention. These machines are capable of automatically filling, sealing, and labeling products. On the other hand, Semi-Automatic Packaging Machinery requires some level of human intervention to operate. Although these machines can automate certain parts of the packaging process, they still rely on manual assistance for tasks such as loading and unloading products.</p></p>
