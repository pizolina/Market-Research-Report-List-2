Comprehensive Refractometer accurately measures the refractive index of lenses and aids in determining the power of spectacles. Focimeter measures the focal length of lenses. Tonometer measures intraocular pressure to detect glaucoma. Fundus Camera captures images of the retina for diagnosis. Slit Lamp Microscope examines the eye's anterior segment. Finally, there are other types of equipment available in the market that cater to specific ophthalmic medical needs.</p></p>
