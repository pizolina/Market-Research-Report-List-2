<p><p>Virtual Private Network (VPN) routers are devices that provide secure and private internet connections by encrypting data and routing it through a virtual private network. The market for VPN routers can be divided into two types: wireless VPN routers and wired VPN routers. </p><p>Wireless VPN routers allow users to connect devices to the internet via Wi-Fi, removing the need for physical connections. They are suitable for homes, small businesses, or remote work setups where mobility is important.</p><p>Wired VPN routers, on the other hand, require physical connections through Ethernet cables. These routers are typically used in larger businesses or organizations that prioritize stability and high-performance internet connections.</p><p>Both types of VPN routers aim to provide secure and private internet access, but they differ primarily in connectivity options and target users.</p></p>
