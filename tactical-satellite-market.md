North America is also a key market for tactical satellites, driven by the presence of major defense organizations and the need for advanced defense capabilities.</p><p>Some of the latest market trends in the tactical satellite market include the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning algorithms for improved data analysis and decision-making.
