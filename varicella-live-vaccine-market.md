<p><p>The Varicella Live Vaccine market is divided into two types: Monovalent Vaccines and Multivalent Vaccines. Monovalent Vaccines are formulated to protect against a single strain of the virus that causes varicella (chickenpox). They are specifically designed for individuals who have not been previously inoculated or have not contracted the disease. On the other hand, Multivalent Vaccines are developed to provide protection against multiple strains of the varicella virus, making them suitable for individuals who require immunity against various strains simultaneously.</p></p>
