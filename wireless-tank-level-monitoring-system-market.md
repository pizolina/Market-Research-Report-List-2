<p><ul><li>Point Level Monitoring</li><li>Continuous Level Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless tank level monitoring systems are used to keep track of the level of liquid or solid materials in tanks, without the need for physical wire connections. There are two types in the market: point level monitoring and continuous level monitoring. Point level monitoring detects when the material reaches a predetermined level, signaling when it is empty or full. Continuous level monitoring provides real-time information about the material level, offering precise measurements throughout the tank's capacity. These systems enhance efficiency and prevent overflows or shortages in various industries that rely on tank storage.</p></p>
