Standalone smartwatches operate independently, with built-in cellular connectivity to enable calls, messaging, and internet access. Hybrid smartwatches combine traditional analog watches with smart features such as fitness tracking and notifications. These different types cater to varying user preferences and provide a range of functionalities to enhance the overall smartwatch experience.</p></p>
