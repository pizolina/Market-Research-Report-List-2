<p><p>The small-scale bioreactor market can be classified into two types: reusable bioreactors and single-use bioreactors. Reusable bioreactors are designed to be cleaned, sterilized, and reused for multiple cycles, offering cost-effectiveness in the long run. On the other hand, single-use bioreactors are pre-sterilized and disposable, eliminating the need for cleaning and reducing the risk of contamination. Single-use bioreactors are easy to handle and can be used for various applications such as cell culture, fermentation, and protein expression.</p></p>
