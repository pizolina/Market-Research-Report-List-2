<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Optical scales are a type of measuring device used in various industries. Incremental optical scales provide relative measurements by generating encoded signals based on the displacement of an object. They are commonly used in applications where continuous monitoring or position control is required. On the other hand, absolute optical scales provide absolute position feedback, allowing precise measurements without the need for recalibration. Absolute optical scales find applications in industries where accuracy is paramount, such as precision engineering and manufacturing. Both types of optical scales play a vital role in ensuring accurate measurement and control in different sectors.</p></p>
