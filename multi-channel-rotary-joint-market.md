<p><p>A multi-channel rotary joint is a device used to enable the transmission of multiple media (such as fluid, gas, or electrical signals) between two rotating parts of a system. Multi-channel rotary joints are commonly used in applications such as automated machinery, robotics, packaging equipment, and medical devices.</p><p>The multi-channel rotary joint market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period.
