<p><p>Doorphone Video Intercom systems are divided into two main types: Direct Video Intercom and Digital Video Intercom. Direct Video Intercom refers to a system where the video and audio communication occurs directly between the doorphone and the indoor unit. It typically uses analog wiring and is easier to install and maintain. Digital Video Intercom, on the other hand, employs digital technology and can transmit video and audio signals over long distances. It offers advanced features such as remote control and connectivity options, but requires more complex installation and setup.</p></p>
