<p><p>Therapeutic Drug Monitoring (TDM) market consists of various types of immunoassay technologies used for analyzing drug levels in patient's blood. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) utilizes light-emitting reactions for detection, while Fluorescence Immunoassay (FPIA) relies on fluorescent labels. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) employs radioactive tracers in measurement. Colorimetric Immunoassay uses color changes for detection. Other Immunoassays encompass different immunoassay techniques. These technologies aid in measuring drug concentrations to ensure optimal therapeutic levels, preventing toxicity or ineffectiveness. Understanding these types helps healthcare professionals choose the appropriate method for precise drug monitoring and adjustment.</p></p>
