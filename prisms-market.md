<p><strong>The Prisms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersion Prisms</li><li>Deviation Prisms</li><li>Rotation Prisms</li><li>Displacement Prisms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prisms are optical devices that refract light, and they are available in various market types. Dispersion prisms are used to separate white light into its component colors. Deviation prisms bend light at a specific angle, redirecting its path. Rotation prisms rotate the image of an object. Displacement prisms shift the position of an image, making it appear in a different location. These market types offer different functionalities for applications such as spectroscopy (dispersion prisms), beam steering (deviation prisms), imaging systems (rotation prisms), and optical alignment (displacement prisms).</p></p>
