<p><p>The Outdoor Air Quality Monitoring System market includes various types of monitoring systems. Passive monitoring systems collect samples over a period of time to determine pollutant levels. Intermittent monitoring systems measure air quality periodically. Manual monitoring involves manual collection and analysis of air samples. Stack monitoring focuses specifically on monitoring pollutant emissions from industrial smokestacks. These different types of monitoring systems cater to different needs and requirements for assessing and managing outdoor air quality.</p></p>
