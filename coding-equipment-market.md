<p><p>Coding equipment is used in various industries to mark products with important information such as batch numbers, expiration dates, and barcodes. There are different types of coding equipment available in the market. Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) printers use small drops of ink for marking on various surfaces. Thermal Inkjet (TIJ) printers use heat to propel ink onto the surface. Thermal Transfer Overprinting (TTO) machines use heat to transfer ink from a ribbon onto the substrate. Laser coders utilize laser technology to create permanent markings. Other types of coding equipment include hot stamping machines, label printers, and dot peen markers, among others.</p></p>
