<p><p>Electronic ballasts are devices used to regulate the electrical current in lighting fixtures. The market for electronic ballasts can be categorized into three main types: T5/T5HO, T8/T8HO, and T12/T12HO. T5/T5HO ballasts are designed for fluorescent lamps with a diameter of 5/8 inch and provide high light output. T8/T8HO ballasts are for fluorescent lamps with a diameter of 1 inch and offer energy efficiency. T12/T12HO ballasts cater to fluorescent lamps with a diameter of 1.5 inches and are gradually being phased out due to their lower efficiency.</p></p>
