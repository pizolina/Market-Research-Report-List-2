<p><p>The polyisobutylene pressure sensitive adhesive market can be categorized into two types: waterborne and solvent-based. Waterborne polyisobutylene adhesives are formulated using water as a carrier instead of solvents. These adhesives offer environmentally friendly solutions, low volatile organic compound emissions, and easy cleanup. On the other hand, solvent-based polyisobutylene adhesives are formulated using solvents as carriers. These adhesives provide quick drying time, excellent adhesion to various substrates, and higher initial tack. The choice between waterborne and solvent-based adhesives depends on the specific application requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
