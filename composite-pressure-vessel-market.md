<p><p>The Composite Pressure Vessel Market is segmented into three types - Type III, Type IV, and Others. Type III vessels are made of a carbon fiber composite overwrapped with metal. They offer exceptional strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Type IV vessels, on the other hand, consist of a plastic liner with a carbon fiber composite overwrap. This type provides superior lightweight properties, making them ideal for automotive and transportation industries. The "Others" category includes alternative designs and materials that do not fall under Type III or Type IV classifications.</p></p>
