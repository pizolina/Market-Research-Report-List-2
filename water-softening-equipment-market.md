<p><p>Water softening equipment refers to devices used to remove minerals such as calcium and magnesium from the water, improving its quality and reducing potential issues like scale buildup. There are two main types of water softening equipment: salt-based and salt-free. Salt-based equipment uses a process called ion exchange to replace calcium and magnesium ions with sodium ions, while salt-free equipment uses methods such as catalytic conversion or template-assisted crystallization to alter the composition of minerals without adding salt. Both types offer effective solutions for water softening, catering to different preferences and needs.</p></p>
