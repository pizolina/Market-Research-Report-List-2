<p><p>Non-narcotic analgesics, also known as pain relievers, can be classified into two main market types: non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) and steroid anti-inflammatory drugs. NSAIDs work by reducing pain and inflammation without the use of steroids, making them suitable for milder cases. Steroid anti-inflammatory drugs, on the other hand, contain corticosteroids which have powerful anti-inflammatory effects and are typically used for more severe conditions. Both types of medications are widely available over-the-counter or through prescriptions and play a crucial role in managing pain and inflammation in various medical conditions.</p></p>
