<p><p>Effervescent tablet manufacturing services market includes various types of manufacturing methods, such as wet granulation, dry granulation, and direct compression. Wet granulation involves the mixing of active ingredients and excipients in a liquid binder, followed by drying and granulating the mixture. Dry granulation involves compacting the powder mixture without using any liquid binder. Direct compression involves directly compacting the powdered mixture without any additional processing steps. These methods are used in the production of effervescent tablets, which are known for their ability to dissolve quickly in water and release active ingredients.</p></p>
