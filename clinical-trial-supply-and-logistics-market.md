<p><p>Clinical trial supply and logistics refer to the management and distribution of supplies, drugs, and equipment required for conducting clinical trials. One such trend is the increasing adoption of direct-to-patient (DTP) logistics solutions, which eliminate the need for patients to visit trial sites, thereby improving patient recruitment and retention rates.
