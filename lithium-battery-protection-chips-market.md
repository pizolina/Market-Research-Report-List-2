<p><p>Lithium battery protection chips are crucial components used in lithium-ion batteries to ensure their safe operation. These chips come in various types, including overvoltage protection (OV), over-temperature protection (OT), and overcurrent detection (OCD). OV chips monitor and control the battery voltage to prevent overcharging, which can lead to battery damage or even explosion. OT chips monitor the battery temperature to avoid overheating and potential thermal runaway. OCD chips detect excessive current flow and protect the battery from damage by disconnecting it from the power source.</p></p>
