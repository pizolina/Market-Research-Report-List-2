<p><p>Interior shading systems refer to window coverings or blinds that are installed inside a building. Vertical installation refers to the orientation of the shading system where the blinds or window coverings are installed vertically. This type of installation is commonly used for sliding glass doors or large windows. Conversely, horizontal installation refers to the orientation of the shading system where the blinds or window coverings are installed horizontally. This type of installation is often used for smaller windows or traditional window shapes. Both vertical and horizontal installation provide different options for controlling light, privacy, and aesthetics in interior spaces.</p></p>
