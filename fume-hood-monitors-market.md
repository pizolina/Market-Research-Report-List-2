<p><p>Fume hood monitors are used to ensure safety in laboratory settings by monitoring air quality and removing hazardous fumes. There are two main types of fume hood monitors: ducted fume hoods and ductless fume hoods. Ducted fume hoods are connected to an exhaust system, which safely removes fumes and contaminants outside the building. On the other hand, ductless fume hoods use filters to remove harmful particles and recirculate clean air back into the laboratory. Both types serve the same purpose of protecting users and the environment from potentially dangerous substances.</p></p>
