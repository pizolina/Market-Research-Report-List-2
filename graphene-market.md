<p><p>The graphene market can be divided into three major types: graphene powder, graphene oxide, and graphene film. Graphene powder refers to the fine particles of graphene, often used in industries like electronics and composite materials. Graphene oxide is a chemically modified form of graphene, known for its good dispersion in solvents and compatibility with other materials. It is widely used in applications like energy storage and biomedicine. Graphene film, on the other hand, consists of a continuous layer of graphene, which finds applications in flexible electronics and transparent conductive coatings.</p></p>
