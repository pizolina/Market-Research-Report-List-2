<p><p>Alternative finance encompasses various market types. Peer-to-peer (P2P) lending enables individuals to lend money directly to borrowers, providing a platform for interest-bearing loans. Crowdfunding empowers entrepreneurs to gather funds from numerous individuals, typically in exchange for rewards or equity. Invoice trading involves the sale of outstanding invoices at a discount to gain immediate funds. Other types of alternative finance include revenue-based financing, where companies repay investors based on a percentage of their revenues, and supply chain finance, which optimizes cash flow between businesses in a supply chain. These emerging markets provide alternative sources of financing outside traditional banking systems.</p></p>
