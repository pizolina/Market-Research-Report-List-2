<p><ul><li>Slit Scanning Laser System</li><li>Spot Scanning Laser System</li><li>Wave-Front Scanning Laser System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Excimer Laser System market consists of three types: Slit Scanning Laser System, Spot Scanning Laser System, and Wave-Front Scanning Laser System. Slit Scanning Laser System employs a narrow beam that moves across the cornea in a linear fashion, whereas Spot Scanning Laser System uses a smaller beam that scans the cornea in various patterns. Wave-Front Scanning Laser System measures irregularities in the eye's optical system and corrects them with a customized laser pattern. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences, providing options for effective and efficient laser surgeries.</p></p>
