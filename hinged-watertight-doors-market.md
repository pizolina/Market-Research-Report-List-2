<p><p>The hinged watertight doors market consists of three types: hydraulic watertight doors, electric watertight doors, and pneumatic watertight doors. Hydraulic watertight doors are operated using hydraulic power, providing high strength and reliability. Electric watertight doors are powered by electricity, offering ease of use and quick operation. Pneumatic watertight doors function using compressed air, ensuring efficient sealing and minimal maintenance. These different types cater to diverse needs in industries where watertight doors are required, such as shipbuilding, offshore oil and gas, and marine transportation.</p></p>
