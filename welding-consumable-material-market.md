<p><p>The welding consumable material market consists of several types of materials used in welding processes. Stick electrodes are coated rods that create an electric arc to join metals. Solid wires are used in gas metal arc welding (GMAW) processes to melt and fuse metals together. Flux cored wires have a hollow center filled with flux, providing better penetration and protection against impurities. Submerged arc welding (SAW) wires and fluxes work together to create a molten pool between the electrode and workpiece, resulting in a strong weld.</p></p>
