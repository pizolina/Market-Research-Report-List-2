<p><p>Particle size distribution measurement instruments are used to determine the size distribution of particles in a sample. The market for these instruments can be categorized into different types. Laser diffraction instruments utilize laser beams to measure the scattering pattern caused by particles, providing information on their size. Dynamic light scattering instruments use the movement of particles in a liquid medium to determine their size distribution. Nanoparticle tracking analysis instruments track individual particles in a liquid to measure their size distribution. Lastly, other instruments may use different techniques such as sedimentation or sieving to determine particle size distribution.</p></p>
