<p><p>The aircraft radars market can be classified into two types: military grade and civil grade. Military grade radars are designed specifically for the defense sector and are used by military forces to detect, track, and identify aircraft, missiles, and other threats. They possess advanced features such as high sensitivity, long-range coverage, and capability to operate in harsh conditions. On the other hand, civil grade radars are primarily used in commercial aviation for air traffic control and weather monitoring purposes. They generally have shorter range and lower power compared to military radars.</p></p>
