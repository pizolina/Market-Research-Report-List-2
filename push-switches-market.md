<p><p>Push switches are categorized into two main types: metal push switches and plastic push switches. Metal push switches are made of sturdy materials such as stainless steel or brass, making them durable and suitable for applications where high durability is required. On the other hand, plastic push switches are made of plastic materials, which make them lightweight and cost-effective. Plastic push switches are commonly used in household appliances, consumer electronics, and automotive industries. Both types of switches serve as momentary contact switches, where an electrical circuit is momentarily closed or opened when the switch is pressed.</p></p>
