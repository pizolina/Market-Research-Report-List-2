<p><p>The WiFi security camera market is divided into two types: local storage and cloud storage. Local storage cameras store video footage directly onto an SD card or a hard drive connected to the camera. These cameras provide the advantage of data privacy and do not require Internet connectivity for recording. On the other hand, cloud storage cameras store video footage on remote servers accessed through the Internet. These cameras offer remote access, allow for easy sharing of footage, and provide backup in case the camera is damaged or stolen.</p></p>
