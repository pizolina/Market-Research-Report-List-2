<p><p>The plastic hot and cold pipes market includes several types of pipes used for carrying hot and cold water in residential and commercial buildings. Cross-Linked Polyethylene (PEX) pipes are flexible and have high temperature resistance. Polyethylene Raised Temperatures (PE-RT) pipes are also highly resistant to heat and are suitable for radiant heating applications. Polypropylene Random Copolymer (PPR) pipes are known for their durability and resistance to corrosion. Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride (C-PVC) pipes are used for hot water applications due to their high heat resistance. Polybutylene (PB) pipes are flexible and easy to install, but they have lower temperature resistance compared to other types.</p></p>
