<p><p>Organofunctional silane resins are a type of hybrid organic-inorganic material. These resins are derived from silanes, which are compounds made up of silicon and hydrogen or carbon atoms. The growing demand for these applications is expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>In the future, the organofunctional silane resins market is forecasted to experience steady growth. Amino functional silanes are used as adhesion promoters and coupling agents in various applications, while glycidoxy functional silanes offer improved adhesion to substrates and enable epoxy curing. Vinyl functional silanes act as crosslinking agents and modifiers, whereas (meth)acryloxy functional silanes are used in UV-curable systems and as adhesion promoters for acrylic-based coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
