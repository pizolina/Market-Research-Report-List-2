<p><p>The oil-filled pumps market includes three main types: vertical, horizontal, and inclined pumps. Vertical pumps are designed with a vertical shaft that allows them to efficiently pump fluids upward or downward. Horizontal pumps have a horizontal shaft and are commonly used for various industrial applications. Inclined pumps, as the name suggests, are set at an inclined angle and are best suited for applications where fluid needs to be pumped from a lower to a higher level. Each type offers specific features and benefits based on the intended use and installation requirements.</p></p>
