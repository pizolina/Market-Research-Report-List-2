<p><p>Airline catering service refers to the provision of meals, snacks, and beverages to passengers on board an aircraft. These services are typically outsourced to catering companies specializing in providing food and beverage solutions to the aviation industry.</p><p>The airline catering service market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The market is primarily driven by the increasing air passenger traffic, particularly in emerging economies. The rising disposable income and changing consumer preferences for premium food offerings have also contributed to the growth of the market.
