<p><p>The optical microscope market consists of three main types: monocular, binocular, and trinocular. A monocular microscope is equipped with a single eyepiece, suitable for individual use and basic applications. A binocular microscope has two eyepieces, enabling a more comfortable viewing experience and better depth perception, making it ideal for educational and professional use. On the other hand, a trinocular microscope has three eyepieces, with an additional port for attaching a camera or other imaging devices, which is beneficial for documentation and research purposes.</p></p>
