<p><p>The acrylic rubber market comprises various types of acrylic rubbers, including active chlorine acrylate rubber, epoxy acrylate rubber, dienes acrylate rubber, and carboxyl type acrylate rubber. Active chlorine acrylate rubber is known for its excellent oil and heat resistance, while epoxy acrylate rubber offers high chemical resistance and good adhesion properties. Dienes acrylate rubber exhibits superior flexibility and low temperature resistance. Carboxyl type acrylate rubber provides exceptional mechanical properties and weather resistance. These types of acrylic rubbers cater to different industrial applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
