<p><p>The Personal Air Sampling Pump market comprises two main types: High Flow and Low Flow pumps. High Flow pumps are designed to collect larger volumes of air, typically for industrial applications where high concentrations of contaminants may exist. These pumps can provide higher flow rates, allowing for a greater detection of airborne pollutants. In contrast, Low Flow pumps are suited for collecting smaller volumes of air, commonly used in occupational hygiene monitoring or indoor air quality assessments, where lower concentrations of contaminants are expected.</p></p>
