<p><p>Circular microfluidic connectors are used to connect and disconnect fluidic systems with circular interfaces, ensuring a reliable and leak-free connection. They are commonly used in applications such as medical devices, biotechnology, and chemical analysis. On the other hand, linear microfluidic connectors are designed to connect channels or microchannels in a straight line, offering a simple and efficient way to connect multiple fluidic components. Both types of connectors play a crucial role in microfluidics by enabling fluid control and manipulation in miniature systems.</p></p>
