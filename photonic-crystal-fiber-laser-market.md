<p><p>The Photonic Crystal Fiber Laser market consists of two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. CW Fiber Lasers emit a continuous beam of light, providing a constant power output. They are often used in applications requiring a stable laser source, such as telecommunications and material processing. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Lasers produce short bursts of light with high peak power, making them suitable for applications like laser marking and micromachining, where precision and high intensity are crucial.</p></p>
