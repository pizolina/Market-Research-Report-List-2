<p><p>Seamless copper tubes are classified into different types based on their specifications and applications. Type K copper tubes are durable and used primarily for underground water service lines. Type L tubes are versatile and commonly used in residential and commercial plumbing systems. Type M tubes are lighter and less expensive, suitable for water distribution and heating systems. DWV (Drain, Waste, and Vent) tubes facilitate the removal of waste and air in plumbing systems. ACR (Air Conditioning and Refrigeration) tubes are designed specifically for HVAC systems. Other types of seamless copper tubes cater to specific industry needs, such as medical and industrial applications.</p></p>
