<p><p>Corrosion resistant reinforcing bars are used in construction to prevent corrosion and increase the longevity of structures. Galvanized reinforcing bar is coated with a layer of zinc. Glass-reinforced-plastic rebar is made of a composite material that is corrosion resistant. Stainless steel reinforcing bar is made of stainless steel, which is naturally resistant to corrosion. MMFX reinforcing bar contains a mix of steel and corrosion-resistant alloy. Low alloy corrosion resistant reinforcing bar is made with a special alloy that provides resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
