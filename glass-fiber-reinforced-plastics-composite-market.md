<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics Composite market is categorized into two types: Thermoplastic Plastic and Thermosetting Plastic. Thermoplastic Plastic refers to a type of composite material that can be easily melted and reshaped multiple times without significant changes to its properties. On the other hand, Thermosetting Plastic is a type of composite material that, once heat-cured, becomes hard and rigid and cannot be reshaped. Both types have their own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
