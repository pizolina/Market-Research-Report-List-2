<p><p>Shotcrete machines are used in construction for applying concrete or mortar through a nozzle onto surfaces. There are two types of shotcrete machines: wet shotcrete machines and dry shotcrete machines. Wet shotcrete machines mix water with concrete or mortar before spraying it onto the surface, while dry shotcrete machines use pre-mixed dry materials and then add water during the spraying process. Both types offer advantages; wet shotcrete machines are ideal for large volume applications, while dry shotcrete machines are more versatile and can be used for various construction projects.</p></p>
