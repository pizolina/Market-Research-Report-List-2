<p><p>The thin film drug market consists of various types. The first type is oral thin film, which involves the administration of drugs using a strip placed on or under the tongue. This method provides quick and convenient drug delivery. The second type is transdermal thin film, which involves the application of a thin patch on the skin to deliver drugs into the bloodstream. This method is often used for slow and controlled drug release. Lastly, there are other thin film drug types that include buccal, sublingual, and ocular thin films, each designed for specific drug delivery purposes.</p></p>
