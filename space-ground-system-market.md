<p><p>The Space Ground System market consists of various types of infrastructure for space operations. Ground stations are used to receive and transmit signals to spacecraft in space. Mission Control Centers are command hubs where operators monitor and control space missions. Ground networks provide connectivity between spacecraft, ground stations, and control centers. Remote infrastructure refers to equipment located in remote areas to support launch and satellite operations. Launch facilities are specialized sites where rockets are launched into space. These components together form the backbone of space operations and enable communication and control of spacecraft.</p></p>
