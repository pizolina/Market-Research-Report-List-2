<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fanfold paper is available in two market types: single layer and multi-layer. In the single layer market, the paper is made up of a single sheet, providing a simple and lightweight solution. It is commonly used for printing receipts, tickets, and labels. On the other hand, in the multi-layer market, the paper consists of multiple sheets interleaved together, offering carbonless copies of written or printed content. This type of paper is often used for invoices, sales orders, and other forms that require duplicate or triplicate copies.</p></p>
