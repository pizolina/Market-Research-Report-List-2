<p><p>The anesthetic gas monitoring market consists of two main types: benchtop and portable monitors. Benchtop monitors are larger devices that are typically used in hospitals and clinics. They are designed to provide continuous and accurate monitoring of anesthetic gases, such as nitrous oxide and sevoflurane, in operating rooms during surgery. On the other hand, portable monitors are smaller and more mobile, allowing for monitoring outside of the hospital setting, such as in ambulances or during transportation of patients. These monitors are convenient and provide real-time data to ensure patient safety and optimal anesthetic administration.</p></p>
