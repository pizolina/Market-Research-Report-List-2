These advancements, coupled with the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms, are enabling robots to perform complex bending tasks with higher precision and speed.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a trend towards collaborative robots (cobots) in bending applications. <p><p>The robotic bending cell market comprises two main types: compact cells and grip cells.
