<p><p>A notebook cooler, also known as a laptop cooling pad, is a device designed to prevent a laptop from overheating and improve its performance by increasing airflow around and under the device. Additionally, the growing awareness about the adverse effects of overheating on laptop performance and lifespan has further driven the demand for notebook coolers.</p><p>Furthermore, technological advancements in notebook coolers, such as the integration of adjustable fan speed and illumination features, have gained significant popularity among users.
