<p><p>Laboratory ion meters are instruments used to measure ion concentration in solutions. There are two main types of ion meters: portable and benchtop. Portable ion meters are compact and can be easily carried to the site of measurement. They are suitable for field applications and quick measurements. On the other hand, benchtop ion meters are larger and more sophisticated, designed for laboratory use. They offer advanced features and higher accuracy, making them ideal for precise measurements in research and industrial settings.</p></p>
