<p><strong>The Mechanical Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Magnetic Encoders</li><li>Rotary Magnetic Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical encoder market comprises linear magnetic encoders and rotary magnetic encoders. Linear magnetic encoders are used to determine linear position, velocity, and direction, commonly in applications like CNC machines and robotics. They utilize a reading head that moves linearly along a scale and measures the magnetic field variation to determine position. On the other hand, rotary magnetic encoders are employed to measure angular position and speed, widely used in motor control, robotics, and industrial applications, featuring a rotating magnetic disk and a sensor that detects the magnetic field changes.</p></p>
