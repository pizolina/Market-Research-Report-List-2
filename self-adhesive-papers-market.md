<p><p>The self-adhesive papers market comprises two main types of printing methods utilized for printing on the adhesive paper surface. The first method is flexographic printing, which involves using flexible relief plates to transfer ink onto the self-adhesive paper through a rotary press. The second method is letterpress printing, wherein ink is transferred from raised surfaces of movable type or plates onto the adhesive paper. These printing methods play a crucial role in the manufacturing and customization of self-adhesive papers for various applications.</p></p>
