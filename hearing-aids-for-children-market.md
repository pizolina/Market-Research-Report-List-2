<p><p>Hearing aids for children are available in various market types to cater to different needs. Behind-the-ear (BTE) hearing aids are worn behind the ear and are suitable for children of all ages. In-the-ear (ITE) hearing aids are custom-made to fit inside the ear canal for a discreet appearance. Hearing implants are surgically implanted devices that provide a more permanent solution for severe hearing loss. There are also other types of hearing aids available, including those with wireless connectivity, rechargeable batteries, or advanced features to enhance speech understanding for children.</p></p>
