<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The LED work lights market consists of two primary types: battery-operated LED work lights and plug-in LED work lights. Battery-operated LED work lights are portable and do not require a direct power source. They are suitable for use in areas without access to electricity or for projects that demand mobility. On the other hand, plug-in LED work lights rely on a direct power source and have a continuous supply of energy. These lights are commonly used in workshops, garages, and construction sites where a consistent power supply is available.</p></p>
