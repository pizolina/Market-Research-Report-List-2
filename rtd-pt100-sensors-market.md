<p><p>The RTD (PT100) sensors market consists of two main types: wire-wound RTDs and thin film RTDs. Wire-wound RTDs are composed of a resistive wire wound around a ceramic or glass core. They offer higher accuracy and stability, making them suitable for precise temperature measurement in industrial applications. On the other hand, thin film RTDs have a thin layer of resistive material deposited onto a substrate. They are more compact, have faster response times, and are commonly used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and medical where space is a constraint.</p></p>
