<p><p>The employee computer monitoring software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing businesses to monitor their employees' computer activities from anywhere and at any time. On the other hand, on-premise software is installed directly on the company's own servers and requires local management and maintenance. While cloud-based solutions offer flexibility and scalability, on-premise software provides more control and data security within the company's premises.</p></p>
