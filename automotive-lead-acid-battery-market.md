<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Lead Acid Battery Market comprises various types such as VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead Acid) Battery and Flooded Battery. VRLA batteries are maintenance-free and sealed, ensuring safety and reducing the risk of electrolyte leakage. They are commonly used in vehicles as they require less space and can withstand harsh conditions. On the other hand, Flooded batteries are unsealed and require periodic maintenance, including adding distilled water. They are preferred for applications that require high power output and are cost-effective compared to VRLA batteries.</p></p>
