<p><p>Semiconductor image sensors are widely used in digital cameras and mobile devices. CCD (charge-coupled device) and CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor) are the two main types of image sensor technologies in the market. CCD sensors use a complex manufacturing process to capture and convert light into electrical signals, resulting in high-quality images but higher power consumption. On the other hand, CMOS sensors are more cost-effective, consume less power, and offer faster image processing. Despite being less sensitive to light, CMOS sensors have gained popularity due to their versatility and lower cost.</p></p>
