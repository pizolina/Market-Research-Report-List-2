<p><p>The synthetic industrial absorbent market is comprised of two main types: polypropylene absorbents and polyester absorbents. Polypropylene absorbents are made from a thermoplastic polymer that can quickly absorb oil, grease, and other hydrocarbon-based liquids. They are commonly used in industrial settings such as oil spills and automotive maintenance. Polyester absorbents, on the other hand, are made from a synthetic fiber that has a high absorption capacity for water-based liquids and chemicals. They are often utilized in chemical spills and laboratory settings.</p></p>
