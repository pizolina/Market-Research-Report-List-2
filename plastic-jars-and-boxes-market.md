<p><p>The plastic jars and boxes market is comprised of various types such as PET, PP, HDPE, and LDPE. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is commonly used for packaging beverages, food, and personal care products due to its durability and clarity. PP (Polypropylene) is known for its heat resistance, making it suitable for microwaveable containers. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is often used for household products, as it is resistant to chemicals and has good impact strength. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and commonly used for squeeze bottles and plastic bags.</p></p>
