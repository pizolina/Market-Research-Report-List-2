<p><p>The foldable drone market can be categorized into two main types: brushed motor and brushless motor. Brushed motor drones are generally more affordable and suitable for beginners, as they rely on a simple motor design with brushes for power transmission. On the other hand, brushless motor drones are more advanced and offer higher performance due to their brushless design, which eliminates the need for brushes and results in increased durability, efficiency, and speed. Both options cater to different consumer needs and preferences within the foldable drone market.</p></p>
