<p><p>Buccal tubes are dental devices used in orthodontic treatments. Smooth bottom buccal tubes have a flat surface on the bottom, which allows for easy attachment to the bracket. This design also reduces friction, providing greater comfort during teeth movement. On the other hand, net bottom buccal tubes have small holes in the bottom surface to enhance the bond between the tube and the tooth. This design increases the adhesive surface area, improving the strength and durability of the attachment. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on individual treatment requirements.</p></p>
