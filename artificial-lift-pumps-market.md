<p><p>The Artificial Lift Pumps market is divided into various types including Electric Submersible Pump (ESP), Rod Pump, Progressive Cavity Pump (PCP), Plunger Lift, and Hydraulic Piston Pump. ESP is a pump system submerged in the well that uses electricity to lift fluids. Rod Pump uses a set of sucker rods and a plunger to lift fluid to the surface. PCP uses a helical rotor within a stator to pump fluid in a progressive manner. Plunger Lift uses gas pressure to lift fluid. Hydraulic Piston Pump uses hydraulic pressure to pump fluid.</p></p>
