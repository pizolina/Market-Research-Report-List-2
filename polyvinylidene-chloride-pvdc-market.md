<p><ul><li>Isotactic</li><li>Atactic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC) is a market type of polymer commonly used as a barrier coating for various applications due to its excellent oxygen and moisture barrier properties. Isotactic PVDC refers to a configuration where all the chlorine atoms in the polymer chain are on the same side, resulting in a regular and highly ordered structure. Atactic PVDC, on the other hand, has chlorine atoms randomly distributed along the polymer chain, leading to a more amorphous and disordered structure. Both isotactic and atactic forms provide different properties, making them suitable for different applications in the PVDC market.</p></p>
