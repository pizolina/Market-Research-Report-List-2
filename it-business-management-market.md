Demand Management focuses on identifying and managing customer requirements and aligning them with business goals. Resource Management ensures optimal utilization of available resources, such as personnel and technology, to meet project demands. Project Portfolio Management aims to prioritize and manage multiple projects, allocating resources efficiently. Lastly, Test Management involves planning, coordinating, and controlling software testing activities throughout the development process to ensure quality and reliability of the final product.</p></p>
