<p><p>N-carbamylglutamate (NCG), also known as Carbaglu, is a synthetic amino acid derivative that plays a crucial role in the urea cycle, a metabolic process responsible for detoxifying ammonia and producing urea. NCG is commonly used as a pharmaceutical drug to treat hyperammonemia in patients with urea cycle disorders.</p><p>The future outlook of the N-carbamylglutamate (NCG) market appears promising.
