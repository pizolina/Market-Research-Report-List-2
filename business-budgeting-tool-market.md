<p><p>Business budgeting tools can be categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based budgeting tools are hosted on remote servers and accessible through the internet. They provide flexibility, scalability, and ease of collaboration as users can access and update budgets from any location. On-premises budgeting tools, on the other hand, are installed and maintained on the user's own servers. They offer more control and data privacy but lack the convenience and accessibility provided by cloud-based solutions.</p></p>
