<p><p>Anticrease agents are used in the textile industry to reduce creases and wrinkles on fabrics. There are two types of anticrease agents: dye-bath lubricants and wet processing lubricants. Dye-bath lubricants are used during the dyeing process to prevent creasing and improve color uniformity. Wet processing lubricants, on the other hand, are used in various stages of wet processing, such as scouring and bleaching, to minimize fabric damage and improve overall fabric quality. Both types of anticrease agents play a crucial role in enhancing the appearance and durability of textiles.</p></p>
