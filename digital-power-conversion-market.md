<p><strong>The Digital Power Conversion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC/DC</li><li>Isolated DC/DC</li><li>Sequencers</li><li>DC/AC</li><li>Hot Swap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital power conversion market consists of various types of power converters. AC/DC converters transform alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC), while isolated DC/DC converters provide galvanic isolation and convert one DC voltage level to another. Sequencers coordinate the powering sequence of multiple power supplies. DC/AC converters perform the opposite function of AC/DC converters by transforming DC to AC. Lastly, hot swap converters enable the safe removal and replacement of power modules without disrupting the operation of the system. These converters play a crucial role in ensuring efficient, reliable, and safe power conversion in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
