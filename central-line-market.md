<p><p>Central line market types refer to the different types of central venous catheters used for various medical purposes. Single-lumen catheters have a single channel, allowing for the delivery of fluids or medications. Double-lumen catheters are equipped with two channels, permitting the simultaneous administration of multiple fluids or medications. Triple-lumen catheters have three channels, enabling the administration of several fluids or medications at once. Apart from these three main types, there are other specialized catheters available in the market, designed for specific medical needs such as hemodialysis or long-term use. These different types cater to the unique requirements of patients across various medical scenarios.</p></p>
