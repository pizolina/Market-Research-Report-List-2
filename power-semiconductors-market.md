<p><p>Power semiconductors market is categorized into various types including diodes, switches, rectifiers, and others. Diodes are electronic devices that allow current to flow in one direction, while switches control the flow of electrical signals. Rectifiers convert alternating current (AC) into direct current (DC). Other power semiconductors encompass devices like thyristors, insulated gate bipolar transistors (IGBTs), and power modules. These semiconductors find applications in diverse industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, industrial, and aerospace. They play a crucial role in power control, conversion, and distribution, contributing to the efficient operation of various electronic systems.</p></p>
