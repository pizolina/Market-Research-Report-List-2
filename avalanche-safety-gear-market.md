<p><p>The avalanche safety gear market can be classified into two main types: airbags and security backpacks. Airbags are devices that inflate rapidly upon activation to create a large volume of low-density material, increasing the person's chances of staying on the surface during an avalanche. On the other hand, security backpacks are designed to provide essential equipment and tools for avalanche rescue, such as shovels, probes, and emergency communication devices. Both types of gear aim to enhance the safety of individuals in the event of an avalanche.</p></p>
