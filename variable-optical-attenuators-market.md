<p><p>The Variable Optical Attenuators (VOA) market can be categorized into three types: Component, Handheld, and Benchtop. </p><p>Component VOA refers to the integration of VOA in other optical devices or systems, allowing control of the output signal power. Handheld VOA is a portable device that provides precise attenuation for in-field applications, making it convenient for technicians to optimize signal power. Benchtop VOA, on the other hand, is a larger and more powerful device commonly used in laboratories or testing environments for accurate and repeatable attenuation measurements. These different types cater to specific needs in various industries and applications.</p></p>
