<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Solar Freezer market consists of two main types: Battery Powered System (BPS) and Solar Direct Drive (SDD). In BPS, the freezer is run by solar energy stored in batteries, allowing for uninterrupted cooling even during cloudy days or at night. On the other hand, SDD directly uses solar energy to power the freezer without the need for batteries, making it more efficient in sunny areas and reducing overall costs. Both systems contribute to sustainable and eco-friendly cooling solutions by reducing reliance on traditional electricity sources.</p></p>
