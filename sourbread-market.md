<p><p>Sourbread can be categorized into two market types: Group Type and Flake Type. Group Type sourbread is typically sold as large loaves with a thick and crispy crust. This type is often preferred by those who appreciate a more rustic and chewy texture. On the other hand, Flake Type sourbread is characterized by its thin and flaky crust, making it easy to tear apart. This type is popular for its delicate and light characteristics, attracting individuals who prefer a softer bread experience.</p></p>
