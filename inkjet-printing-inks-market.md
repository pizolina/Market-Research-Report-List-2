<p><ul><li>Drop On Demand (DOD) Inkjet Inks</li><li>Continuous Inkjet Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet printing inks market types include Drop On Demand (DOD) and Continuous Inkjet Inks. DOD inkjet inks are designed to be selectively ejected onto the printing surface using an electric pulse, resulting in high-quality images and precise printing. Continuous inkjet inks, on the other hand, involve a continuous flow of ink which is then selectively deflected to create characters or images. Both types offer their own advantages, allowing for efficient, reliable, and versatile printing solutions in various industries.</p></p>
