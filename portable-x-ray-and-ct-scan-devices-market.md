<p><p>Portable X-ray and CT scan devices are medical equipment used for diagnostic imaging purposes. The market is categorized into two types: One-Piece Type and Split Type. </p><p>One-Piece Type devices are compact and all the components, including the generator, tube, and control panel, are integrated into a single unit. They are easy to maneuver and require minimal setup, making them suitable for urgent or emergency situations.</p><p>In contrast, Split Type devices consist of separate components, such as the generator and the X-ray tube. This allows for greater flexibility in positioning the equipment, making them ideal for larger medical facilities or when specific imaging requirements need to be met.</p></p>
