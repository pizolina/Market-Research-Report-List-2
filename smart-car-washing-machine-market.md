<p><p>The smart car washing machine market is divided into two types: contact and contactless. A contact car washing machine requires physical contact between the machine's brushes or rollers and the car's surface to clean it. On the other hand, a contactless car washing machine utilizes high-pressure water jets and cleaning agents to safely remove dirt and grime without touching the car's surface. Both types of machines offer efficient car washing solutions, but their methods differ in terms of physical contact.</p></p>
