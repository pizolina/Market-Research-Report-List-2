<p><p>Industrial surge protectors are devices used to protect electrical equipment and sensitive electronic devices from power surges. There are four main types of industrial surge protectors: plug-in devices, hard-wired devices, line cord devices, and power control center market. Plug-in devices are easily inserted into wall outlets and provide surge protection for specific equipment. Hard-wired devices are permanently installed into an electrical system to protect all connected devices. Line cord devices are designed to protect individual power cords. Lastly, power control centers offer comprehensive surge protection for a large number of electrical devices within a facility.</p></p>
