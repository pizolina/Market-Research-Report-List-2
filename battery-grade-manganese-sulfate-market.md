In the reduction market, Manganese Sulfate is produced through a reduction process, which involves reducing manganese dioxide with a reducing agent like sulfur dioxide. Both types of processes yield battery-grade Manganese Sulfate, suitable for use in lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
