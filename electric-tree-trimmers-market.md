<p><p>The electric tree trimmers market consists of three main types: battery-powered, electric, and hybrid. Battery-powered tree trimmers are cordless and operate using rechargeable batteries, offering convenience and flexibility. Electric tree trimmers are corded and require an electrical outlet for power, providing continuous operation but limiting mobility. Hybrid tree trimmers combine the features of both battery-powered and electric trimmers, offering the option to switch between battery and corded operation. These different types cater to various user preferences and needs in terms of power source and mobility.</p></p>
