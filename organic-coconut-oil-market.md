<p><p>The organic coconut oil market is segmented into two types: Virgin Organic Coconut Oil and Refined Organic Coconut Oil. Virgin Organic Coconut Oil is obtained from fresh coconuts via a cold-pressed extraction process, preserving its natural aroma and flavor. It is known for its higher nutrient content, including antioxidants and vitamins. Refined Organic Coconut Oil is made from dried coconut meat and undergoes refining processes like bleaching and deodorizing, resulting in a neutral taste and higher smoke point. Both types meet organic standards, but virgin oil retains more natural qualities.</p></p>
