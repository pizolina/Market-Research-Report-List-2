<p><p>Air Admittance Valves (AAVs) are devices used in plumbing systems to help control the flow of air and prevent the backflow of sewer gases. There are three types of AAV markets based on pressure conditions - Zero Pressure, Positive Pressure, and Negative Pressure markets. In the Zero Pressure market, AAVs are mainly used in wastewater drainage systems where there is no pressure present. In the Positive Pressure market, AAVs are designed to handle pressure conditions in the plumbing system. Conversely, in the Negative Pressure market, AAVs are used to regulate air intake under negative pressure conditions to prevent siphoning and backflow.</p></p>
