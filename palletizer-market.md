<p><p>The Palletizer market consists of two main types: Traditional Palletizers and Robotic Palletizers. Traditional Palletizers are mechanical systems that use various mechanisms like conveyors, lifts, and arms to stack products onto a pallet manually. On the other hand, Robotic Palletizers use robotic arms with advanced sensors and software to automatically stack products onto pallets. While Traditional Palletizers are widely used and have lower upfront costs, Robotic Palletizers offer greater flexibility, speed, and efficiency, making them more popular in industries with higher production volumes and diverse product types.</p></p>
