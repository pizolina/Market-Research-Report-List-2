<p><p>The heat-resistant rubber hose market comprises two major types: natural rubber and styrene butadiene rubber (SBR). Natural rubber hoses are manufactured using natural latex derived from rubber trees, making them highly resilient and flexible even under high temperatures. They possess excellent abrasion resistance and are commonly used in industrial applications. On the other hand, SBR hoses are synthetic rubber hoses that offer good heat resistance, chemical resistance, and durability. They are widely used in automotive and construction industries where heat resistance is crucial.</p></p>
