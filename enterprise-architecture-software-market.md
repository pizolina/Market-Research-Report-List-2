<p><p>Enterprise Architecture Software is classified into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-premises. </p><p>Cloud-Based architecture software refers to software that is hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet. It offers flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access the software from anywhere, anytime, and eliminating the need for expensive infrastructure setup and maintenance. </p><p>On-premises architecture software, on the other hand, is installed and operated on the company's own servers or computer systems. It offers greater control and security over data but requires significant upfront investment in hardware and ongoing maintenance.</p></p>
