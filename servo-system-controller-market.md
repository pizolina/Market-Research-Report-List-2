<p><strong>The Servo System Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Axis Controller</li><li>Multi-Axis Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Servo System Controller market is mainly divided into two types: single-axis controller and multi-axis controller. A single-axis controller is designed to control the movement of a single servo motor. It is commonly used in applications where only one motor needs to be controlled, such as in CNC machines or robots with a single joint. On the other hand, a multi-axis controller is capable of controlling the movement of multiple servo motors simultaneously. It is often used in complex systems that require coordinated movements of several motors, like industrial automation or robotic assembly lines.</p></p>
