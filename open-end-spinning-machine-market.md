<p><strong>The Open-end Spinning Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-venting Type</li><li>Pumping Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The open-end spinning machine market is classified into two types: self-venting type and pumping type. A self-venting type machine harnesses a suction system to remove the yarn from the nozzle, enhancing productivity and reducing yarn breakage. On the other hand, a pumping type machine uses compressed air or a fan to drive the yarn into a collection device. The pumping type is usually more cost-effective and appropriate for producing larger volumes of yarn. Both types play crucial roles in the open-end spinning machine market, catering to various production needs and requirements.</p></p>
