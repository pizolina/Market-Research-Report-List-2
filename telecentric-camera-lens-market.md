<p><p>Telecentric camera lens market types include Object Square Telephoto Lens and Bi-Telecentric Lens. Object Square Telephoto Lens is designed to capture square-shaped objects without any perspective distortion, making it ideal for industrial metrology and robotics applications. Bi-Telecentric Lens provides telecentricity in both the object and image space, ensuring accurate image measurements and eliminating perspective errors. This lens is commonly used in dimensional measuring systems, inspection systems, and machine vision applications. Both lens types offer precise and distortion-free imaging solutions for specific industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
