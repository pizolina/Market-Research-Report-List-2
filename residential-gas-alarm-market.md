<p><p>The residential gas alarm market can be classified into two main types: stationary gas alarms and portable gas alarms. Stationary gas alarms are installed in fixed locations within the home, such as near gas appliances or in basements, and are designed to detect and alert residents of potential gas leaks. Portable gas alarms, on the other hand, are portable devices that can be carried around the house or even taken outside, providing individuals with personal protection against gas leaks, regardless of their location. Both types of gas alarms play a crucial role in ensuring residential safety by detecting and preventing gas-related hazards.</p></p>
