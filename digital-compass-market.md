<p><p>The digital compass market consists of three types: 3-axis, 6-axis, and 9-axis. The 3-axis type uses sensors to measure magnetic field data in three dimensions. The 6-axis type combines the 3-axis feature with an accelerometer to also measure linear acceleration. The 9-axis type adds a gyroscope to the 6-axis model, enabling it to measure angular velocity as well. These market types cater to different needs, with the 3-axis type being simpler, the 6-axis type more versatile, and the 9-axis type providing the highest level of accuracy and functionality.</p></p>
