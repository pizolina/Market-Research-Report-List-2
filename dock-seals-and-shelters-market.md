<p><p>Dock seals and shelters are essential equipment that provide a secure, weatherproof seal between a loading dock and a vehicle during the loading or unloading process. There are three types in the market: Mechanic dock seals and shelters use foam-filled pads to create a tight seal, ensuring energy efficiency. Inflatable dock seals and shelters use airbags to create a seal, easily accommodating varying trailer sizes. Sponge dock seals and shelters use compressible foam to create a flexible seal, suitable for irregular trailer sizes. Each type offers different advantages based on specific docking needs.</p></p>
