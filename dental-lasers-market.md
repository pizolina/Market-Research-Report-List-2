<p><strong>The Dental Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Tissue Dental Lasers</li><li>All Tissue Dental Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental lasers are classified into two main types: Soft Tissue Dental Lasers and All Tissue Dental Lasers. Soft Tissue Dental Lasers are designed specifically for procedures that involve the soft tissues of the mouth, such as gum contouring and treating periodontal disease. These lasers emit a wavelength that is highly absorbed by the water in soft tissues, allowing for precise and efficient tissue removal with minimal bleeding and discomfort. On the other hand, All Tissue Dental Lasers can be used on both soft and hard tissues, making them versatile tools for a wide range of dental procedures, including cavity treatment, gum surgery, and root canal disinfection.</p></p>
