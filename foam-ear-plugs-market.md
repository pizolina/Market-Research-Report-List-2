<p><p>Foam ear plugs are available in two main types: corded and cordless. Corded foam ear plugs are equipped with a string or cord that connects the two plugs, making it easier to keep them together and prevent loss. These plugs are commonly used in environments where the ear plugs need to be removed and reinserted frequently. On the other hand, cordless foam ear plugs do not have a cord and are preferred in situations where the wearer needs uninterrupted hearing protection, reducing the risk of entanglement or interference.</p></p>
