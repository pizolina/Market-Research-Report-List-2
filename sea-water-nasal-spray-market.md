<p><p>The Sea Water Nasal Spray market is divided into two types: Isotonic and Hypertonic. Isotonic Sea Water Nasal Spray has a salt concentration similar to that of human body fluids, allowing for gentle and soothing nasal irrigation. It helps maintain the normal moisture and cleanliness of the nasal passages. On the other hand, Hypertonic Sea Water Nasal Spray has a higher salt concentration, which aids in reducing nasal congestion and clearing mucus. It is particularly useful for individuals with severe congestion or allergies. Both types provide relief for nasal discomfort but differ in salt concentration and their respective benefits.</p></p>
