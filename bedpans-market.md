Several factors contribute to this market growth. Firstly, the rising geriatric population and an increase in chronic illnesses requiring long-term bed rest are driving the demand for bedpans. Additionally, the growing prevalence of diseases such as urinary incontinence and mobility-related disorders further boosts the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in bedpan design and materials have improved comfort and convenience for both patients and healthcare providers. The outbreak has increased the awareness of hygiene and infection control measures, creating a surge in demand for disposable bedpans.
