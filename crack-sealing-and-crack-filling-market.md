<p><p>Crack sealing and crack filling are commonly used methods in pavement maintenance and preservation. On the other hand, crack filling involves the application of a fill material, such as asphalt emulsion, to the cracks in order to prevent further expansion and damage.</p><p>The crack sealing and crack filling market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. Cold-applied thermoplastic bituminous materials are applied at ambient temperatures and can be used for smaller cracks. Hot-applied thermoplastic bituminous materials are heated and have better adhesion properties, making them suitable for larger cracks. Chemically cured thermosetting materials are self-leveling and provide long-lasting crack repair. These materials are used to seal and fill cracks in pavements and prevent further damage.</p></p>
