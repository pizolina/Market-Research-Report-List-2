<p><p>The industrial hand gloves market consists of two main types: reusable gloves and disposable gloves. They provide long-term hand protection and are ideal for applications requiring frequent glove changes. On the other hand, disposable gloves are designed for single-use only and are made from materials like latex, vinyl, or nitrile.
