<p><ul><li>Transparent PET film</li><li>Translucent PET film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PET Film is widely used in the cable industry due to its excellent electrical insulation properties. Transparent PET film is a type of PET film that allows complete visibility through the film, enabling efficient inspection of cables. On the other hand, translucent PET film is a partially transparent film that allows light to pass through but diffuses it, providing a softer appearance. Both types cater to different market needs, with transparent PET film facilitating visual inspection and translucent PET film providing aesthetic appeal while maintaining insulation properties.</p></p>
