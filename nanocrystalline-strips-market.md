<p><p>Nanocrystalline strips are a type of magnetic material that possess high saturation flux density and low core losses. Nanocrystalline strips find application in power distribution transformers, current transformers, inductors, and other electronic and electrical devices.</p><p>The global market for nanocrystalline strips is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
