<p><p>The Civil Night Vision System market comprises two main types: Low Light Night Vision System and Infrared Night Vision System. A low light night vision system amplifies the available light to enhance visibility in dark or low-light conditions. On the other hand, an infrared night vision system uses thermal imaging technology to detect the heat emitted by objects and create a visible image based on the differences in temperature. Both types play a crucial role in providing improved vision and situational awareness in various civil applications.</p></p>
