Endopeptidases break down proteins internally, targeting peptide bonds within proteins. Exopeptidases, on the other hand, break down proteins externally, focusing on the terminal amino acids. Other proteases may have unique characteristics or functions that make them suitable for specific applications. These different types of proteases provide options for feed manufacturers to choose the most effective enzyme for their specific animal feed formulation.</p></p>
