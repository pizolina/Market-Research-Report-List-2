<p><p>Fly fishing bags can be categorized into two main types: chest bags and backpacks. They are ideal for anglers who prefer to have their gear within arm's reach. On the other hand, backpacks are larger bags worn on the back, offering more storage space for fishing gear, food, and personal items. Backpacks are suitable for anglers who require extra storage capacity and are planning longer fishing trips.</p></p>
