<p><p>Oilfield fracturing chemicals are chemicals used in the hydraulic fracturing process in oilfields. Hydraulic fracturing, also known as fracking, is a technique used to extract oil and gas from underground reservoirs. These fractures then allow the oil and gas to flow more freely and be extracted.</p><p>The future outlook of the oilfield fracturing chemicals market is promising. <p><p>The oilfield fracturing chemicals market includes various types of chemicals used in the oil and gas industry. Clear brine fluids are used to control formation pressure and stabilize wellbore conditions. Shale inhibitors help prevent the swelling and dispersion of shale formations. Lubricants are used to reduce friction during drilling operations. Non-emulsifiers prevent emulsion formation during production processes. H2S scavengers remove hydrogen sulfide gas, which is toxic and corrosive. Defoamers reduce foam formation in oilfield fluids. Surfactants are used for emulsification, wetting, and dispersing purposes in various oilfield applications.</p></p>
