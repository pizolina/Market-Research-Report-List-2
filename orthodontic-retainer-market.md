<p><p>The orthodontic retainer market offers different types of retainers with various materials. Metal retainers are made of stainless steel and are highly durable and long-lasting. They are often visible in the mouth. Ceramic retainers are tooth-colored, providing a more aesthetically pleasing option. They are more prone to breakage and require extra care. Polymer materials are used for transparent or invisible retainers. They are comfortable and convenient but may not be as durable as metal retainers. The market offers these options to cater to individual preferences and needs.</p></p>
