<p><p>Plastic membrane market is categorized into several types including PVA, BOPP, LDPE, PET, PA, and CPP. PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) is widely used due to its excellent water solubility and biodegradability. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) offers high clarity and good moisture barrier properties. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is known for its flexibility and durability. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is commonly used for packaging applications due to its strength and transparency. PA (Polyamide) exhibits high mechanical strength and resistance to abrasion. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) provides good heat resistance and sealing properties, making it suitable for food packaging.</p></p>
