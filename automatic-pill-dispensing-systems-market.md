<p><p>Automatic pill dispensing systems are advanced devices used for dispensing medications in a timely and controlled manner. The market is categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic systems are designed to automate the entire medication management process, including sorting, dispensing, and notifying the patient when it's time to take a dose. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require manual assistance from the patient or caregiver for loading medication, while the rest of the process remains automated. These systems ensure medication adherence, reduce errors, and promote independence in medication management.</p></p>
