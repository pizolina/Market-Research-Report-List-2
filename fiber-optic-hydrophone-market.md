<p><p>A fiber-optic hydrophone is a device used for detecting underwater sound waves or pressure fluctuations by converting them into optical signals. It consists of an interferometric sensor made of a coiled or stretched fiber-optic cable, which can measure the strain induced by the acoustic pressure.
