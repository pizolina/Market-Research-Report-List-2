<p><ul><li>Radar Control</li><li>Procedural Control</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military aircraft air traffic control equipment market can be categorized into three main types. Firstly, radar control involves using radar systems to detect and track aircraft in real time. Secondly, procedural control relies on predetermined routes and instructions for aircraft movement without radar assistance. Lastly, the "Other" category includes various technologies like automated dependent surveillance-broadcast (ADS-B), ground-based augmentation systems, and satellite-based navigation systems. These different types of equipment serve the purpose of ensuring safe and efficient air traffic management for military aircraft operations.</p></p>
