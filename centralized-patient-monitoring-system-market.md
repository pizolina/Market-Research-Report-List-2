Centralized Patient Monitoring Systems offer the advantage of continuous monitoring and early detection of deteriorating patient conditions, thereby improving patient outcomes and reducing hospital readmissions.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in wireless communication technologies, the Internet of Things (IoT), and cloud computing have enhanced the capabilities of these systems, enabling seamless connectivity, real-time data transmission, and data analytics.
