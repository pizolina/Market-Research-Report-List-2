<p><p>The belt grinder market consists of two types: 2-wheel grinders and 3-wheel grinders. 2-wheel grinders are equipped with two grinding wheels that are usually placed side by side. These grinders provide more stability and control when grinding materials. On the other hand, 3-wheel grinders have three grinding wheels, with one wheel serving as a drive wheel and the other two functioning as idler wheels. This design enables more flexibility in terms of belt tension and tracking, making it easier to achieve precise grinding results.</p></p>
