<p><p>Neuron tracing software market is classified into three types based on dimensions - 2D, 3D, and 4D. </p><p>2D software allows the reconstruction of neuronal structures from 2D images by analyzing their connectivity and features. It provides a basic representation of the neurons' shape and organization. </p><p>3D software offers a more in-depth analysis, reconstructing neurons in a three-dimensional space. This enables researchers to visualize and analyze complex neuronal networks and their connections. </p><p>4D software goes a step further by adding a temporal dimension, allowing the tracking and observation of dynamic changes in neurons over time. This facilitates the study of neuronal development and activity.</p></p>
