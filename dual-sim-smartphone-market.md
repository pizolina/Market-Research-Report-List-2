<p><p>A dual SIM smartphone refers to a mobile device that can support two SIM cards simultaneously. There are two common types of dual SIM smartphones: dual standby and dual standby dual active. In the dual standby market, the device has two SIM slots but can only actively use one SIM card at a time for making calls or accessing data. On the other hand, in the dual standby dual active market, both SIM cards can be actively used simultaneously, enabling users to make calls or use data from both SIM cards concurrently.</p></p>
