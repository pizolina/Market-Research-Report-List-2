<p><p>The piezoelectric market can be categorized into three main types: ceramics, composites, and polymers. Ceramics, such as lead zirconate titanate (PZT), are widely used due to their high piezoelectric efficiency and robustness. Composites combine ceramic particles with polymers or metals to enhance flexibility and durability while maintaining good piezoelectric properties. Polymers, like polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF), offer advantages such as low manufacturing cost, high flexibility, and compatibility with large-area applications. Each market type has its unique characteristics and applications, catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
