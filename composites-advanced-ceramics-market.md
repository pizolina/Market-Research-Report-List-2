Carbon materials are ceramics that are composed primarily of carbon, offering excellent thermal and electrical conductivity. The "other" market segment comprises ceramics that do not fall into the fiber or carbon categories, such as oxides or silicates. These ceramics usually exhibit unique properties and are used in a wide range of applications.</p></p>
