<p><p>The IPS display market comprises various types of displays, including AH-IPS, E-IPS, H-IPS, and PLS. AH-IPS (Advanced High-Performance IPS), known for delivering accurate colors and wide viewing angles, is commonly used in high-end monitors and TVs. E-IPS (Enhanced IPS) is widely adopted due to its cost-effectiveness and decent performance. H-IPS (Horizontal IPS) provides better color accuracy and response time but is less commonly used. PLS (Plane-to-Line Switching) is a Samsung-developed IPS technology known for its high brightness and energy efficiency. These different IPS display types cater to various consumer needs in the market.</p></p>
