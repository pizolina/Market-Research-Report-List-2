<p><p>High Temperature Grease & Lubricants market can be classified into two types based on the thickener used. Soap Thickener is a widely used type that combines metallic soaps with mineral oil. It has good stability, high load carrying capacity, and resistance to oxidation. On the other hand, Non-Soap Thickener is a newer type that uses inorganic or organic substances, such as polyurea or bentonite, as thickeners. These greases offer excellent thermal stability, resistance to water washout, and oxidation resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications.</p></p>
