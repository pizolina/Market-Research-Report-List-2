<p><strong>The High Frequency Surface Mount Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire Wound Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Surface Mount Inductors market includes three types: Wire Wound Type, Film Type, and Multilayer Type. Wire Wound Type inductors are constructed using a coiled wire, providing high power and excellent performance at low frequencies. Film Type inductors utilize a conductive film, offering low DC resistance and good performance at high frequencies. Multilayer Type inductors consist of multiple layers of conductive tracks, providing high inductance values and efficient operation at high frequencies. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the market for high frequency surface mount inductors.</p></p>
