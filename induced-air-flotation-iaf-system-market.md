<p><p>Induced Air Flotation (IAF) system is an advanced wastewater treatment technology that utilizes fine air bubbles to separate and remove suspended solids, oil, and other contaminants from water. The system works by dissolving air under pressure and releasing it at atmospheric pressure in the flotation tank, creating microbubbles that attach to the particles in the water, causing them to float to the surface for removal.</p><p>The Induced Air Flotation (IAF) system market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
