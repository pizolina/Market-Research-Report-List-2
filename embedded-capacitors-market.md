<p><p>Embedded capacitors are passive electronic components that are integrated directly into a printed circuit board (PCB) or substrate. With the growing demand for smaller and more efficient electronic devices, the need for compact and high-performance components like embedded capacitors is increasing. These capacitors offer several advantages such as reduced size and weight, improved reliability, and enhanced electrical performance.
