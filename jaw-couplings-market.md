<p><p>Jaw couplings are a type of mechanical coupling used to connect two shafts in order to transmit torque and rotational motion. They consist of two hubs with curved jaws that interlock, creating a flexible connection. This design allows for some misalignment and dampens vibrations, making jaw couplings popular in various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and power generation.</p><p>The future outlook for the jaw couplings market is positive, with a projected CAGR of 4.8% during the forecasted period.
