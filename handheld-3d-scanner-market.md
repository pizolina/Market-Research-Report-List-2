<p><p>The handheld 3D scanner market is divided into two main types: the Structure Light Scanner and the Laser Scanner market. Structure Light Scanner uses a projected light pattern and a camera to create a 3D model of an object, capturing its surface details and texture. On the other hand, Laser Scanners employ a laser beam to measure the distance between the scanner and the object, generating a precise and accurate 3D representation. Both scanners offer portable and user-friendly solutions for various applications such as engineering, manufacturing, and healthcare.</p></p>
