<p><p>Vacuum sintering and degreasing integrated furnace is a type of industrial furnace that combines both sintering and degreasing processes in a single unit. Sintering is the process of compacting and forming powdered materials into solid shapes using heat and pressure, while degreasing involves removing any oils or contaminants from the surface of the materials before sintering. This integrated furnace offers cost and time efficiencies by eliminating the need for separate furnaces for each process.</p><p>The vacuum sintering and degreasing integrated furnace market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors such as the increasing demand for sintered components in automotive, aerospace, and healthcare industries. Sintered components are widely used in these industries for their high strength, precision, and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>Moreover, the growing focus on energy efficiency and environmental sustainability is driving the adoption of vacuum sintering and degreasing integrated furnaces. These furnaces operate in a controlled vacuum environment, which reduces energy consumption and emissions compared to traditional furnaces.
