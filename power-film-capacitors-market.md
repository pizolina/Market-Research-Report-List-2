<p><p>Power film capacitors are classified into several types based on the film material used. PPS film capacitors utilize polyphenylene sulfide film, known for its high temperature resistance, making them suitable for applications where high reliability is required. PET film capacitors employ polyethylene terephthalate film, providing excellent moisture resistance and stable capacitance. PEN film capacitors utilize polyethylene napthalate film, offering high voltage and high frequency capabilities. Lastly, the "Others" category includes capacitors made from different film materials, such as polyimide or polycarbonate, based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
