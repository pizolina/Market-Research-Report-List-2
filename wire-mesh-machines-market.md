<p><p>Wire mesh machines are used for manufacturing wire mesh products such as fences, filters, and screens. Automatic wire mesh machines are fully automated and require minimal human intervention, making the production process fast and efficient. Semi-automatic wire mesh machines, on the other hand, require operators to manually load and feed the wire, but have automated functionalities for cutting and bending. While automatic machines are suitable for high-volume production, semi-automatic machines offer more flexibility and customization options. Both types contribute to the growing wire mesh machines market, offering solutions for various industrial needs.</p></p>
