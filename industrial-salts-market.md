<p><p>The industrial salts market comprises two main types: brine and salt mines. Brine is a concentrated solution of salt that is obtained through solution mining or solar evaporation of seawater. It is commonly used in the chemical industry for the production of chlorine, soda ash, and caustic soda. On the other hand, salt mines involve extracting solid salt deposits from underground mines. These salts are widely used in various industries such as food processing, water treatment, and de-icing applications. Both brine and salt mines play a significant role in meeting the global demand for industrial salts.</p></p>
