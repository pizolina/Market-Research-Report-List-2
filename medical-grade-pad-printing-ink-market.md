<p><strong>The Medical Grade Pad Printing Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>UV Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical grade pad printing ink is used in the healthcare industry to print precise and durable markings on medical devices and equipment. There are two types of inks commonly used: solvent-based and UV curing. Solvent-based inks contain solvents that evaporate as the ink dries, resulting in a permanent print. UV curing inks use ultraviolet light to cure and dry the ink quickly, creating a durable and hard finish. Both ink types are designed to meet strict medical standards for safety, reliability, and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
